## Typography Rules

1. **Max 2 families per project** — display + body. Single-family with weight variation is also valid
2. **Weight hierarchy:** Display 600-800, Body 400, Labels 500, Data mono
3. **Letter-spacing:** Headlines tight (-0.02em to -0.01em), Body normal (0), Small caps wide (+0.05em)
4. **Line-height:** Headlines 1.1-1.2, Body 1.5-1.6, UI labels 1.3
5. **Never:** Arial, Helvetica, Times New Roman, Comic Sans, Papyrus for web projects
6. **Test:** Every pairing at 14px body and 48px display before committing
