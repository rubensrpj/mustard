## Palette Construction Rules

1. **Start from the domain** — What colors exist naturally in this product's world?
2. **One dominant accent** — Multiple accents dilute focus
3. **Semantic colors are non-negotiable** — Success (green), Warning (amber), Error (red), Info (blue)
4. **Test at extremes** — Dark mode, light mode, high contrast, colorblind simulation
5. **Gray is never neutral** — Warm grays (stone/zinc) or cool grays (slate/gray) set the mood
